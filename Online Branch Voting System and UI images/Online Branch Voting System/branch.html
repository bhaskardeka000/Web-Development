Branches

Computer Science And Engineering(CSE)

It is a branch of engineering that integrates the field of computer science and electronic engineering. The various speciality of the branch are software engineering, web development, cyber security etc. The various employment fields are science, technology, industry, exploration.

Civil Engineering(CE)

It is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment. It is considered to be the second oldest disciplines of engineering. It is the application of physical and scientific application to solve a problem of society.

Mechanical Engineering(ME)

It is a branch of engineering that is a combination of engineering physics and mathematics. It generally deals with the principles of material science, analyse and manufacture of a product etc. It is one of the oldest department of engineering.

Electronics And Communication Engineering(ECE)

It is a branch of engineering which deals with the study of various circuits. It can also be said as a sub branch of electrical engineering. It basically deals with the study of semiconductor technology.

Electrical Engineering(EE)

It is a branch of engineering that deals with the study, design and application of electrical equipment. It consists of the study of electromagnetism, electrostatics etc. Activity sector of the branch are electronics, circuit, machine etc.
